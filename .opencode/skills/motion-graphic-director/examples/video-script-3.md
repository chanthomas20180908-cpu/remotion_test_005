# Video Script 3 — Brand Gravity in the AI Age

## Segment Breakdown

### [0:00 - 0:04] Hook — Funnels Are Dead
"Traditional marketing funnels that we've been taught are dead. Today gravity is what'll build you a 100 million dollar winning product."

### [0:04 - 0:12] The Gravity Metaphor
"But what's gravity? Think of your target audience as comets flying through space and your product as a planet. Now, the stronger your gravity, the more you pull them"

### [0:12 - 0:28] Defining Brand
"into your orbit. And that gravity isn't built through funnels or ads, it's built through brand. And by brand I don't mean your logo or that aesthetic you copied from Pinterest. It is the sum total of your entire user experience. Something that emotionally resonates."

### [0:28 - 0:32] The Core Question
"So how do you actually build gravitational pull in the AI age?"

### [0:32 - 0:40] Rule 1 — Sell the Feeling
"First stop selling your product start selling the feeling. Linear doesn't sell project management software, they sell the feeling of finally being in control of your roadmap."

### [0:40 - 0:48] Rule 2 — Tiny Delightful Features
"Then build tiny features that make people smile like Vercel's deployment animations, Spotify Wrapped and Duolingo's passive aggressive notifications."

### [0:48 - 1:00] Rule 3 — Authenticity & BTS
"Third, be authentic and show BTS. Buffer, Basecamp and PostHog are the best case studies that have nailed it. When everyone else is polished and corporate, authenticity becomes your competitive advantage."

### [1:00 - 1:12] Rule 4 — Strong Point of View
"Finally have a strong point of view of the world. Because if you're for everyone, you're for no one. Glossier's beauty in real skin campaign is what made it unique. Superhuman believes email is broken and charges $30/month to fix it."

### [1:12 - 1:20] Closer
"The goal isn't just to get customers, it's to get customers who orbit around you. And once you have it, you don't compete on price or features."
